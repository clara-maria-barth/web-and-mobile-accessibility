Textual Description

The graph depicts the average temperature from 1880 to the present, which has ranged between -0.5 and 1.0 degrees Celsius. Between 1900 and 1920, the lowest point was reached. Following that, the temperature gradually increased, with a brief peak around 1950, followed by a brief drop before rising again. Finally, by 2020, the temperature had risen by around 1 degree Celsius.
